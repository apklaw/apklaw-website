Professional Notice

The regulations governing the legal profession in India impose certain restrictions on advocates with respect to solicitation and advertising of professional services. Accordingly, this website contains only limited information concerning the practice.

The material made available here is intended solely for general informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice, nor should it be construed as an invitation or solicitation to engage professional services.

Further information regarding the practice, including areas of work and professional engagements, may be made available upon request. Visitors seeking such information or wishing to make professional enquiries may write to info@apklaw.in
